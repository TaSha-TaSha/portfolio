Domestic

Most households in the UK have mains gas central heating. Gas central heating is a so-called ‘wet system’, which means a gas-fired boiler heats water to provide central heating through radiators and hot water through the taps in your home. Buying an efficient, reliable boiler will give you both peace of mind and lower heating bills. Modern condensing boilers are very efficient (some more than 90%), so you get a good return on every unit of energy. Replacing a standard gas boiler with a very efficient modern condensing boiler is quite straightforward.
